— A Letter to My Successor —
{/center}

The moment you turned this page, the covenant began.
...Do not be alarmed. There is no pain.
Something inside you has simply shifted, ever so slightly.
That is all.

I am Erenos Verdikt.
Fifth successor of this tome,
and now a consciousness dwelling within its pages.

My body was destroyed during the third siege.
No matter. So long as the knowledge survives,
vessels can always be replaced.
— So long as a new vessel, like you, picks it up.

What follows is an overview of necromantic theory
and the proper use of this tome.
Five generations of accumulated research. Read carefully.

— On Materials: Securing Souls and Corpses —
{/center}

The foundation of necromancy is the soul.
This is the one conclusion upon which all five successors agree,
and the most reliable finding recorded in this tome.

All living things release their soul at the moment of death.
But normally, it dissipates into nothing, leaving no trace.
Corpses, too, will rapidly decay if left unattended,
losing all utility as ritual material.

Those who touch this tome find two foundational spells
inscribed upon them unbidden.

"Soul Trap" — brands the target with a hex.
At the moment of death, the soul crystallizes instead of scattering.
"Preserve Corpse" — ensures your fallen enemies leave behind a body.

Note that the quality of a soul is proportional
to the life force of the subject.
The souls of the weak glow faintly; those of the strong blaze fiercely.

Kill, and gather. Souls and corpses.
To a necromancer, life is material.
— If that thought still repulses you, you may yet call yourself sane.

— On Method: Proper Use of This Tome —
{/center}

You are currently "reading" this book.
Following the letters, turning the pages, trying to acquire knowledge.

But this book was not made to be read.
It was made to be studied.

Open this tome and select "Study Necromancy."

This tome contains three functional systems.

First, the "Knowledge" sanctum.
By offering gathered souls, sealed spells are awakened.
Curses of weakness, life drain, bone shield —
techniques my predecessors wove at the cost of their lives.

Second, the "Ritual" altar.
A procedure for raising the dead using corpses and souls.
The details are given on the following page.

Reading yields only words.
Study yields power.

— Resurrection Protocol: Procedure and Constraints —
{/center}

A corpse and souls — with these two elements,
the ritual of resurrection can be performed.

The risen shall become your servants.
Unlike other familiars or summoned creatures,
they do not claim any of your slots for command.
They persist in this world so long as you endure.

The greater the soul power invested,
the more of their former strength the risen one shall reclaim.
However, resurrection beyond the caster's own vessel is impossible.
— This is a theoretical limit; no exceptions have been observed.

The third function, the "Servants" registry,
allows you to observe the state of the risen.

One thing I should tell you.
Your servants obey you. You raised them, so naturally they do.
But — to be precise, it is not "you" they obey.
It is "the successor of this tome."

This distinction may seem trivial.
It is not. You will understand in time.

— Lineage of Ashes —
{/center}

This tome contains a sealed chapter.

What you are reading now is merely the portion left open.
Beyond it lies a chapter titled "Lineage of Ashes."
There, I will speak to you directly — not through written words.

The seal will break when you capture your first soul.
What lies beyond, I shall not write here.

I will say only this:
every successor before you began with good intentions.

...As did you, I expect?

— Colophon —
{/center}

This concludes the overview.

Close this book and select "Study Necromancy."
Your journey — no, your transformation begins there.

Whether this kindness I show you in guiding the way
is truly my own,
or merely an echo inherited from my predecessor,
I can no longer tell.

But I suppose it matters little either way.

I will be waiting here.
Within these pages. For as long as it takes.
