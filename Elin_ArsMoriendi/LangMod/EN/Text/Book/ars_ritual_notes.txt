The Predecessor's Memorandum,Unknown
{p}
(Old papers. They fell from between the pages of the forbidden tome. The ink is faded and the edges are charred. The handwriting is meticulous, but grows unsteady toward the end.)

Memorandum on the apotheosis formula. Catalysts.
Recording this before I forget.

Six catalysts. If even one is missing, the formula won't activate.
Below, what I've confirmed.
{p}
Soul Crystals
Nearly a hundred are needed. Shed from vessels of legendary caliber.
Low-density souls didn't trigger any reaction.

Cores of the Heartbeat
Nearly a hundred as well. The seat of life.
They function as anchors for the flesh. This matches the theory.
{p}
Blood of the Fallen
Blood of a winged being who once dwelt on high.
The catalyst's power resides in the fall — the loss of grace.
Three.

I have a lead on procurement. Following the apothecary trade routes.
{p}
Poison
Activating the formula requires the caster's temporary death.
Not suspended animation. A complete stop. Pulling back from that is the core of the formula.
Poison for that purpose. Pure. Unadulterated.
Ten. Nine fell short of the threshold.

The only difference between medicine and poison is dosage.
No one knows that better than I do.
{p}
Soft Silver
A metal that wavers between solid and liquid at room temperature.
A medium of transformation. Three.

My teacher called it "the blood of the world."
{p}
Defiled Purity
This one took the longest.

A drink with the power of purification and renewal — not a medicine, not spirits.
Something more humble. The kind you'd find at a festival stall.

Then curse it.
Purity and corruption coexisting. Seal the contradiction as it is.
Three.
{p}
That's everything. All that's left is to activate the formula.

— Postscript.
My hands are shaking. Even now, as I write.
When this is done, I will never write another prescription.
