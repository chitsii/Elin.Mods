This mod adds:
- Necromantic spells
- An undead servant system
- A psychological horror-themed main quest (~2 hours of gameplay)

---

Those who take up the forbidden grimoire "Ars Moriendi — The Art of Dying" gain the power to capture souls, raise the dead as eternal servants, and ultimately transcend humanity itself.

You are the sixth inheritor. The five who came before all began with good intentions. All of them changed.
Are the writings they left in the book teachings — or corruption?

"The difference between 'self-erosion' and 'education'? If you think that distinction matters, you still have much to learn."
— Erenos Verdict, Fifth Inheritor


[h2]Features[/h2]

◆ Soul & Corpse Collection
Brand enemies with a spell, slay them, and harvest their souls and corpses.
Collected souls fuel both spell unlocks and servant resurrection.

◆ Servant Resurrection
Perform a resurrection ritual using a corpse and souls to raise a permanent undead servant.
Any character is eligible — including uniques and bosses.
A dedicated minion system that doesn't consume pet slots.

◆ Necromantic Spells
Offer souls to progressively unlock 16 spells.
From branding, draining, summoning, and warding — to "Soul Chain" which redirects lethal damage to your servants, and "Domain of Death" which deals sustained AoE damage while healing allies.
(Spell balance is still subject to tuning.)

◆ Servant Enhancement
Boost your servants' stats, or graft extra arms and heads using corpses as materials.
But push too far and they may go berserk — awakening, turning hostile, or self-destructing. It's all up to chance.


[h2]Main Quest: "The Unhallowed Path" — 10 Chapters[/h2]

A psychological horror questline depicting your fate as an inheritor of the forbidden book.

In exchange for power, pages begin to fill with writing in your own hand — writing you don't remember.
Karen Gravorn, the "Ash Baroness" of the Jure Order's Templar Knights, appears with a warning and tells of the five predecessors' fates — one who resurrected his dead wife, one who pursued the pinnacle of knowledge, one who died for faith.

All of them were consumed by the book.
Can you resist the grimoire's curse, fight the Templar Knights, and survive as an inheritor?

And then — the ritual of Apotheosis, to transcend humanity.
Will you embrace overwhelming spiritual power and walk the dark path, or refuse?


[h2]Characters[/h2]

- Erenos Verdict
The fifth inheritor. His consciousness persists within the book.

Karen Gravorn
Ash Baroness of the Jure Order's Templar Knights.
A long-lived Elea who survived three sieges against Erenos.

Hecatia
An undying merchant sealed within the book.
Summon her anytime from the grimoire to purchase necromancy scrolls.


[h2]How to Play[/h2]
- After subscribing, the grimoire appears in the black market. (Head straight to Port Kapul!)
- Reading the book activates the necromancy system, main quest, and more.
- Middle-click the book and select "Study Necromancy" to open a dedicated grimoire-style UI.
  Unlock spells, resurrect/manage/enhance servants, and advance quests — all from this screen.
- Since servants are separate from pets, a dedicated HP/MP/Stamina HUD widget is included. Toggle it on/off from the grimoire UI.

[h2]FAQ[/h2]

Q. How do I uninstall?
A. Place the book on the ground and select "Uninstall" from the middle-click menu. This safely removes all servants, NPCs, flags, and spell elements from your save data. Then disable the mod.

Q. The balance feels off!
A. If you have specific feedback, please leave a comment.

Q. I found a bug!
A. Please let me know in the comments. Attaching your Player.log would be a huge help.
