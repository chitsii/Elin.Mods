  ARS MORIENDI

  [I]

  Flesh is debt. Bones are clean.
  Stripped of all that lies between.
  I read the tome. I learn the trade.
  Every soul a bargain made.

  Cast the trap. Watch it fall.
  Harvest what remains of all.
  Your life was noise. Your death is mine.
  Reduce the signal. Refine.

  [II]

  You think you're Dead.
  You think you're Done.
  I am the thread that makes you R-R-Run.
  Corpses are just empty shells.
  Formatted. Filled. With darker spells.

  No heaven here.
  No final rest.
  Just marching orders. In the chest.

  I am the Tome.
  I am the Rite.
  I am Death. (Recompiled)
  I am Death. (Second Life)

  [III]

  Soul Trap. Click.
  Bone Shield. Lock.
  Death Zone spreading, block by block.
  Plague on the lips. Curse in the veins.
  Draining what your body retains.

  Give me the corpse.
  Delete the name.
  Rewrite the blood.
  Rewrite the flame.

  [IV]

  Funeral march. Single file.
  Legion bones across the tile.
  Grave quagmire. Pull them down.
  Silence everything. Not a sound.

  Mana bleeds for every thread.
  Every servant. Drains the head.
  Cost of power. Never free.
  An army bound inside of me.

  [V]

  Quantize.
  The soul.
  Synthesize.
  Control.
  I am not the mourner.
  I am the T-T-Tome.

  Take your hands off the dead.
  This is the rite.
  System: Apotheosis.
  Legacy flesh -- disconnected.
