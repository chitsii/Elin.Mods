"In this arena, everyone begins as a worthless lump of meat, and most end as nothing more..."

West of Lesimas, South of Vernis. 
Within a twisted rift between dimensions, the arena stands with its maw wide open, waiting. 
You are nothing but "meat" — Rank G, the lowest of the low.

With each victory, with each spray of blood, the masks of your allies begin to crack.
The old warrior's obsession. The receptionist's secret. The merchant's hunger.
Under the gaze of Astaroth, the Dragon God who rules this domain—what will you become within his "plan"?

[h3]Special Arena Rules[/h3]
- No Death Penalty
Defeat in the arena costs nothing. No lost skill XP. No lost gold.
A proving ground where you can try any strategy. Challenge as many times as you dare.

- The Battlefield Bites Back
In higher-rank battles, the "Audience" interferes.
Bombardment, elemental scars, healing restrictions — you must overcome the environment itself, not just the boss.

- Boss Transformations
Some bosses change form based on remaining HP.
The same tactics won't work throughout. Adapting your strategy means the difference between life and death.

- Power at a Price
Powerful arena-exclusive items are available, but they cost karma or inflict debuffs.
"Hunger Amulet," "Painkiller," "Forbidden Stimulant" — misuse them, and you destroy yourself.

[h3]How to Install[/h3]
1. Install the required mod "Custom Whatever Loader (CWL)":
https://steamcommunity.com/workshop/filedetails/?id=3544010094

2. Subscribe to this mod. "The Rift Arena" will appear on the world map.

3. Enter it and the event begins automatically.
   Accept "Promotion Matches" from the old warrior and raise your gladiator rank.

[h3]FAQ[/h3]
Q. Too much dialogue!
A. Hold Ctrl to fast-forward.

A. It's designed as a puzzle. Hint: [spoiler]Try using a certain scroll you can exchange for at the Ecopo...[/spoiler]

Q. I want to uninstall.
A. Talk to Iris and use (Mod Settings) to run the uninstall process.
